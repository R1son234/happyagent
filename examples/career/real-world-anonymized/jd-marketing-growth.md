# Anonymized Marketing / Growth JD Pack

This anonymized pack combines recurring requirements from marketing, growth, brand, and campaign operations roles.

## Responsibilities

- Plan and execute integrated marketing campaigns across content, social media, community, partnership, and paid channels.
- Translate product positioning and user insights into clear campaign narratives, launch plans, and reusable marketing assets.
- Build data-driven growth experiments, track funnel metrics, analyze conversion changes, and iterate campaign strategy.
- Coordinate with product, sales, design, operations, and external partners to deliver campaigns on schedule.
- Maintain campaign dashboards and post-campaign reviews so learnings can be reused by the team.
- Use AI tools to improve research, copywriting, content production, audience segmentation, and workflow efficiency.

## Requirements

- 3+ years of marketing, growth, brand, content, or campaign operations experience.
- Strong writing ability and good judgment for positioning, storytelling, and channel-specific content.
- Familiarity with common marketing metrics such as impressions, CTR, leads, conversion rate, CAC, retention, and ROI.
- Ability to manage cross-functional projects and turn ambiguous goals into executable campaign plans.
- Data-driven mindset, structured review habits, and willingness to use AI tools to improve productivity.

## Bonus Signals

- Experience in technology, SaaS, consumer internet, finance, education, or AI-related products.
- Portfolio of campaigns, launch plans, content calendars, research reports, or growth experiments.
- Ability to build lightweight automation for marketing research, material organization, reporting, or content iteration.
